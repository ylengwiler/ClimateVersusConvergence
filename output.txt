Climate Protection versus Economic Convergence?
Author: Yvan Lengwiler

This skript downloads the data and runs the key regressions.
The skript produces regular standard errors in the regressions.
For the paper, EViews was used with the option to compute
Huber-White heteroscedasticity adjusted standard errors. The
reported significance levels are therefore different in the
skript and in the paper.

The paper is available at
https://doi.org/10.5451/unibas-ep85420
